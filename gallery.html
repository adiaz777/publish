Javascript Photo Gallery

This Page features a photo gallery I made using the skills I have gained through a Web Fundamentals class I took at Saint Paul College. The gallery displays some of my favorite pictures of Hobbiton, a village from Lord Of The Rings. I imagine spending a cozy time in this dear village where I can learn to read the astrological signs of fellow Hobbits. All the photos were found on Pixabay.com

In the film Lord Of The Rings, the ficitonal village of Hobbiton is located in The Shire, which is home to many hobbits. However, the actual movie set is now a theme park located in Waikato, New Zealand. The theme park welcomes visitors for a guided tour of the set!
